About This Site

This website shows single family home sales in the past 6 weeks, grouped by neighborhood. I find that Zillow's home sale maps are too cluttered. Also, Zillow doesn't have a way to group by neighborhood. So I set out to make a more dedicated home sales map. This all came from a desire to see if the real estate market is heating up or not.


The sale dates are obtained from padctn.org. That site has some basic information on properties in Nashville, including the last sale date. I pulled a baseline of data in May 2023. I check padctn.org about once a week for updates. If the last sale date for a property has changed from the baseline , then I know there was a sale.
