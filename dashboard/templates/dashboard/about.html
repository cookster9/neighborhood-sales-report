About This Site

I wanted more recent view of home sales than what Zillow could show.
Also, I wanted something grouped by neighborhood. So I made this.

The big icons are Nashville neighborhoods. Click them to get a list of home sales.
The smaller icons are the sold homes.

It currently only shows Single Family home sales

The data is obtained from padctn.org on a weekly basis
