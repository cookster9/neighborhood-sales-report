About This Site

This website shows single family home sales in the past 6 weeks, grouped by neighborhood. I found that Zillow's home sale maps were too cluttered. Also, it was hard to tell if homes are in the same neighborhood or not. So I set out to make a more specific map. It all came from a desire to see if the real estate market is moving right now or not.


The sale dates are obtained from padctn.org. That site gives some basic information on properties in Nashville, including the last sale date. I got a baseline of data in May 2023. I check padctn.org about once a week for updates. If the last sale date for a property has changed from the baseline , then I know there was a sale.
